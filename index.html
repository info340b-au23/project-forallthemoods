For All The Moods

Problem & Data Domain

To bridge this gap and elevate the music experience, our project aims to create a user-friendly and intuitive platform that empowers users to explore music based on their emotional state. The challenge we aim to address in our project is to create a platform that is attuned to each user’s emotional state and ensuring that the music they discover is deeply personal. Creating this platform includes curating mood-based playlists and developing and incorporating interactive elements and visualizations that may improve the overall user’s experience.
